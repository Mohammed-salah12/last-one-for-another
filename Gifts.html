Gifts
Discover the wonderful feeling of joy that comes with helping kids and families. Celebrate the things that matter to you with gifts that make a real and lasting difference. When you give Gifts of Joy like education for girls, helping people with special needs, caring for the sick and making children happy, you're helping make a powerful difference in the world's poorest and most dangerous places. Gift services for the poor can be a great way to spread kindness and support those in need, and you can also share your love by giving a gift. By choosing a gift through this catalog, you are making a donation to One For Other that supports one or more specific program areas.
