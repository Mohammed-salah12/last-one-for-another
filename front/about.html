About Us

Who Are we


we are a group of people that live in gaza, our purpose is to hone society's energy to help the people that suffer in gaza due to: ( unemployment, Lack of job opportunities,disability, etc. )

Our mission


Bringing Hope to the extremely poor (those living on less than $1 a day) , bringing a smile to poor people

Our strategic plan


Getting rid of extreme poverty in Gaza by collecting donations in smart electronic ways and distributing them fairly to the needy and making continuous and permanent initiatives to enhance the spirit of belonging in society.
